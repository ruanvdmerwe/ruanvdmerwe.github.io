A partnership with **Maskew Miller Learning** to bring personalised, CAPS-aligned
tutoring to South African learners through the channel they already use every day:
WhatsApp. Featured on CapeTalk; now used by over 100,000 learners across the country.

The system orchestrates multiple models in real time under mobile-first constraints,
grounding responses in curriculum content while keeping latency and cost workable
at scale.
